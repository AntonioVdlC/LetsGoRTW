About

Let's Go RTW! allows you to find round-the-world itineraries departing from Europe filtered by budget, duration, and departing country and month.

Everyone has dreamt about travelling around the world at one point, and building up a viable itinerary is one of the first challenges of any world traveller! The goal of Let's Go RTW! is to display possible itineraries according to your constraints!

How does it work?

All the itineraries are hand-crafted using uMap. The data used to create those itineraries come mainly from WikiTravel, Google Flights, Rome2Rio and Seat61. The budget is an estimate based on the countries visited and the cost of transportation between cities.

You are free to use, copy or clone any itinerary available on this website!

The itineraries haven't been tested in real life and are just displayed for inspiration. If you find any errors or you think of any improvements to an itinerary, please don't hesitate to send a mail to itineraries@letsgortw.eu!

The man behind this!

Let's Go RTW! was developed in a couple of weeks, right after Antonio came back from a 6-month world trip! Antonio loves planning trips and so he decided to create a little website where he could share itineraries with other travellers and hopefully inspire them to take the trip of their lifes!

Antonio is quite a shy person and therefore he doesn't like to talk a lot about himself ... and talking in the third person really freaks him out, so if you want to know more about me don't hesitate to have a look at my Twitter, my LinkedIn or my world-tour blog!
